Frequently Asked Questions

What is SourceCalc?

SourceCalc is a free Chrome extension that calculates your real profit margin when sourcing products from 1688.com. It automatically reads the product price, adds shipping, customs duties, and platform fees, and shows you the actual profit per unit — all without leaving the 1688 product page.

How does SourceCalc auto-detect prices on 1688?

SourceCalc reads the price directly from the 1688.com product page DOM. When you visit any product page, it automatically extracts the listed price (including tiered pricing) and uses it as the sourcing cost in the profit calculation. No copy-pasting needed.

What costs does the profit calculation include?

The calculation includes: product sourcing cost (auto-detected from 1688), CNY to USD conversion at real-time exchange rates, international shipping (sea or air freight), customs duties and tariffs for your destination country, and platform-specific selling fees for Amazon FBA, Amazon FBM, Shopify, Temu, eBay, or TikTok Shop.

Is SourceCalc free?

Yes, SourceCalc is free during beta. No signup or account required. Install from the Chrome Web Store and it works immediately on any 1688.com product page.

What countries and platforms are supported?

Destination countries: USA, UK, Germany, Japan, Australia, UAE, Saudi Arabia. Selling platforms: Amazon FBA, Amazon FBM, Shopify, Temu, eBay, TikTok Shop. Each combination uses accurate shipping rates and platform fee structures.
