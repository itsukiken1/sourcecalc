## Growth Tactics for the First Month

1. **Follow and engage with 10-15 accounts daily** in the Amazon FBA / sourcing space. Reply with thoughtful comments, not "nice post!" Generic replies get ignored.

2. **Find tweet threads about "how I source from China"** and reply with a genuine tip + mention that you built a free tool. Not spammy — only when truly relevant.

3. **Post a short screen recording** (15-30 sec) showing SourceCalc in action on a real 1688 page. Video tweets get 10x the engagement of text-only.

4. **Cross-post to Reddit** (r/AmazonFBA, r/FulfillmentByAmazon, r/ecommerce) but read each sub's self-promotion rules first.

5. **Consider a Product Hunt launch** after accumulating some Twitter social proof (even 50-100 followers helps).

6. **Quote-tweet relevant content** from popular Amazon FBA accounts and add your perspective. This puts you in front of their audience.
